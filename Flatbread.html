Mediterranean Flatbread

For this Mediterranean flatbread, spread ready-made flatbread with a layer of cream cheese, top with garlicky spinach, artichoke hearts, Kalamata olives, and feta, then bake briefly in a very hot oven. It's a quick, delicious appetizer built on convenience.

Ingredients

  • 1 piece flatbread, about 6x12 inches, such as StonefireÂ® Artisan Flatbread, thawed
  • 1 (3 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
  • 1/2 pound frozen spinach, thawed and squeezed dry
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried dill
  • 1 pinch salt, or to taste
  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 5 marinated artichoke hearts, halved, or more to taste
  • 10 Kalamata olives, pitted and halved, or more to taste
  • 1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil, or to taste

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C). Line a sheet pan with parchment paper.
  1. Place flatbread in the center of the prepared pan and spread with cream cheese.
  1. In a small bowl, combine spinach, garlic, nutmeg, and dill. Season with salt.
  1. Spoon spinach mixture over cream cheese, separating any clumps, and press mixture down slightly into cream cheese. Sprinkle parsley on top.
  1. Place artichoke hearts evenly over spinach layer, and add Kalamata olives around artichoke hearts. Sprinkle with feta cheese.
  1. Bake in the preheated oven until heated through, 8 to 10 minutes.
  1. Remove from the oven and drizzle with olive oil. Cut into 8 triangles.
